Oatmeal

My special recipe for the wet goopy gloppy good good

Much better than the three bears and that golden haired bimbo ever had.

Ingredients

  • Steel cut oats
  • Oat milk
  • Banana
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Sliced Almonds
  • Chia seeds
  • Honey

The How To Do's

  1. Boil the milk
  2. Start cooking oats
  3. Mash banana/add honey
  4. Mix in nuts
  5. Add oatmeal to nut and nana mix
  6. Enjoy
